# 📊 Marketing Campaign Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and marketing campaign performance using Python and Streamlit. The goal is to uncover customer insights, identify high-value customer segments, and recommend data-driven marketing strategies.

The project includes:

- Data Cleaning
- Feature Engineering
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Interactive Streamlit Dashboard
- Business Recommendations

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Businesses often invest heavily in marketing campaigns without understanding which customers are most likely to respond.

This project aims to answer:

- Which customers spend the most?
- Does income affect spending?
- Which customers respond to marketing campaigns?
- How can customers be segmented for targeted marketing?

## 📈 Key Findings

- Most customers belong to low and middle-income groups.
- Higher-income customers generally spend more.
- High-spending customers are more likely to respond to campaigns.
- Age has only a slight influence on campaign response.
- Purchase frequency positively impacts total spending.

---

## 👥 Customer Segmentation

### High-Value Customers

- High Income
- High Spending
- High Purchase Frequency

### Potential Customers

- Medium Income
- Medium Spending

### Price-Sensitive Customers

- Low Income
- Low Spending

### Inactive Customers

- High Recency
- Low Purchase Activity
